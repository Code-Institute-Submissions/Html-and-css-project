The Launch

The appolo 11 mission began with the saturn V vehicle being launched at 9:32 a.m. EDT july 16 1969. It was launched from Kennedy Space Center's Launch Complex Pad 39A, with a crew of three men Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin and MIcheal Collins inside the command module.At 12:22 EDT the engines of the third stage of the saturn v ignite to lift the command module out of earth orbit sending it towards the moon.
